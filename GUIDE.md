# EliteSoftware Client Simulator - User Guide

This guide will walk you through all the features of the EliteSoftware Client Simulator and how to use them.

## 1. Introduction

The EliteSoftware Client Simulator is a rich, interactive application that drops you into the role of an employee at a fictional company called "EliteSoftware." You'll interact with AI-powered coworkers, manage projects, handle emails, and experience a dynamic, simulated corporate environment.

## 2. Getting Started

### Authentication

*   **Login:** When you first launch the application, you'll be presented with a login screen. You can use the pre-configured credentials or create a new user.
*   **Create User:** If you create a new user, you'll need to provide a username and password.

## 3. The Interface

The application is divided into several main views, which you can access from the sidebar on the left.

*   **Inbox:** Your email client.
*   **Directory:** A list of all employees (both users and AI coworkers).
*   **Org Chart:** A visual representation of the company's organizational structure.
*   **Schedule:** A calendar for managing events and deadlines.
*   **Messenger:** An instant messaging client for real-time chat.
*   **Projects:** A view for managing and tracking projects.
*   **Social Media:** An internal social media feed.
*   **Admin:** (Visible to admin users only) A panel for managing the simulation.

## 4. Core Features

### 4.1. Email (Inbox)

*   **View Emails:** The inbox displays a list of your email threads. Click on a thread to view the emails within it.
*   **Compose & Send:**
    *   Click the "Compose" button to open a new email window.
    *   You can write a new email or reply to an existing one.
    *   As you send emails, AI coworkers will realistically reply and interact with you based on their personalities.
*   **Deleting Threads:** You can delete an email thread by clicking the trash icon next to it.

### 4.2. Directory & Org Chart

*   **Directory:** Browse a list of all employees. You can see their name, role, department, and email.
*   **Org Chart:** Visualize the reporting structure of the company.

### 4.3. Schedule

*   **View Events:** The schedule displays a calendar with all company events.
*   **Create Events:** If you are an admin, you can create new events.
*   **Deadlines:** The system automatically creates hidden deadline events for tasks and projects.

### 4.4. Instant Messenger

*   **Conversations:** The messenger view shows a list of your conversations.
*   **Send Messages:** Select a conversation and type your message at the bottom.
*   **AI Interaction:** AI coworkers will participate in group chats and reply to your messages in real-time.

### 4.5. Projects

*   **View Projects:** This view lists all the projects in the company.
*   **Create Projects:** As an admin, you can create new projects, assign a brief, and add members. When a project is created, an AI-generated kickoff email is automatically sent.
*   **Project Interaction:** AI coworkers who are members of a project will proactively send emails with updates, ask questions, and contribute to the project.
*   **Project Deadlines:** When a project with a deadline is created, the system sets up a trigger to have an AI submit the completed project on time.

### 4.6. Social Media

*   **Feed:** The social media view displays a feed of posts from everyone in the company.
*   **Create Posts & Comments:** You can create your own posts and comment on others.
*   **AI Activity:** AI coworkers will post, comment, and like posts, creating a lively internal social network.

## 5. Admin Features

If you are logged in as an administrator, you will have access to the **Admin** view, which allows you to:

*   **Manage Users:** Create, update, and delete user accounts.
*   **Manage Coworkers:** Create, update, and delete AI coworker profiles. You can define their personality, role, relationships, and more.
*   **Send Broadcasts:** Send a company-wide email broadcast.
*   **Manage Company Profile:** Edit the company's tagline and rules, which will influence AI behavior.
*   **Import/Export Data:** Save and load the entire state of the simulation to a JSON file.
*   **Total Reset:** Reset the entire application to its initial state.
*   **Generate Random Content:** Use AI to generate new random coworkers, projects, and events to keep the simulation interesting.

## 6. The Simulation Engine

The heart of the application is its AI-powered simulation engine.

*   **Time:** The simulation has its own internal clock, which you can see in the header. Admins can advance time manually. The simulation can be paused and resumed.
*   **AI Behavior:** AI coworkers are not just reactive. They will proactively:
    *   Send spontaneous, off-topic emails to each other.
    *   Contribute to projects they are on.
    *   Post and comment on social media.
    *   Reply to emails and instant messages.
*   **Dynamic Environment:** The combination of user actions and proactive AI behavior creates a dynamic and unpredictable environment where new stories and interactions can emerge organically.

Enjoy your time at EliteSoftware!
